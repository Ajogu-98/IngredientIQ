Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Overview

IngredientIQ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at ingredientiq.app (the "Service").

By using IngredientIQ, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Do Not Collect

IngredientIQ is designed with privacy in mind. We do not:

  • Require you to create an account or register
  • Collect your name, email address, or any personal identifying information
  • Store the ingredient lists or product names you enter
  • Store images you upload for analysis
  • Track you across other websites
  • Sell your data to third parties

How Your Ingredient Queries Are Processed

When you submit an ingredient list, product name, or image for analysis, that content is sent to Anthropic's Claude AI API for processing. The analysis is returned to your browser in real time. We do not retain copies of your queries or the AI's responses on our servers beyond what is required to complete your request.

Please refer to Anthropic's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle data sent to their API.

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, our hosting provider (Netlify) may automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our site, including:

  • Your IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

This information is used solely for server administration, security, and aggregate analytics. It is not linked to any personal identity.

Cookies

IngredientIQ does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Our hosting provider may set essential cookies required for site functionality and security. We do not use cookies for behavioral advertising or cross-site tracking.

Third-Party Services

Our Service uses the following third-party services:

  • Netlify — website hosting and serverless functions (Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI-powered ingredient analysis (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts — typography (Privacy Policy)

Children's Privacy

IngredientIQ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
